---
title: Exports
---

You can export your chat threads as MarkDown or JSON files.
Click on the <Icon icon="ellipsis" iconType="solid" /> ellipsis icon on the Chat section of Dora to display the options floater.
This option floater is for your current chat thread.
From there click on either "Save as MarkDown" or "Save as JSON" to export your current chat thread.

<Frame>
    <img src="/assets/images/dora/chat/exports1.png" />
</Frame>

Alternatively, you can click on the chat threads dropdown marked with the <Icon icon="caret-down" iconType="solid" /> downward arrow.
This will trigger a floater with all your chat threads. Next to each thread is an <Icon icon="ellipsis" iconType="solid" /> ellipsis.
Click on the ellipses to display the chat options floater. From there, click on the format you want to export in.
